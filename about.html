Welcome to Katherinescravings! I'm Katherine, an undergraduate student at the University of California, Berkeley! I'm currently studying business and history, but in this decal, I'm getting the chance to explore the world of design as well. I love food, and what better way is there to celebrate the intersection between food and design than by creating a website about my food blogging adventures? From the San Gabriel Valley to Hong Kong to Seoul, I've tasted amazing food - it is, after all, a language of love. I began blogging and posting food two years ago to initially compile a photo gallery of all the home-cooked foods I would miss when I started college. However, my account quickly grew into a colorful, delicous collection of all the diverse cultures I tasted at home and away, and celebrated these cultures by sharing them on social media. My next goals are to get better at cooking (especially since I'll be moving out of the dorms into an apartment - from the frying pan into the fire) and gradually work myself up to Yelp-elite status!
